DHQ Value (0-10,000) is a dynasty trade value computed from YOUR league's actual data.

How it's calculated:
• 40% Production — weighted PPG from the last 5 seasons of your league's scoring settings
• 25% Age curve — position-specific peak windows (RBs peak 22-27, QBs 24-34, etc.)
• 20% Situation — roster status, role, trajectory, durability
• 10% Scarcity — supply vs demand at the position in your league
• 5% Peak bonus — years of prime production remaining

Then blended 75% DHQ engine + 25% FantasyCalc market consensus to anchor against the broader dynasty market.
